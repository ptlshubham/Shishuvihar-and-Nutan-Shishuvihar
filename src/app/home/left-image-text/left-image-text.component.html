History
Shishuvihar and Nutan Shishuvihar

The late Charotar Education Society founder, Motibhai Amin Sahib, founded this Shishuvihar with nine children on June 16, 1943 AD. At the end of the year, there were 23 children. Day by day, the reputation of society increased due to its outstanding educational activities. Shishuvihar Nutan Shishuvihar of the Charotar Education Society has flourished like a banyan tree in Junior and Senior KG for seven years, with the chirping of a thousand children. Donations are required for the construction of this complex.
